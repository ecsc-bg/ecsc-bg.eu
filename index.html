Cyber Security Challenge Bulgaria 2023

The European Cyber Security Challenge is an initiative by the European Union Agency for Cybersecurity (ENISA) and aims at enhancing cybersecurity talent across Europe and connecting high potentials with industry leading organizations.

CERT.BG together with cybersecurity enthusiasts is organizing the Cyber Security Challenge in Bulgaria.

When?

The first qualification round starts:

Begin: Wed, 01 April 2023 18:00 East European Time
End: Thur, 01 Jun 2023 18:00 East European Summer Time

Who?

The official requirements are to be of age between 14-25. The qualifications will be separated in two categories:

  • Category Junior (age 14-20)
  • Category Senior (age 21-25)
